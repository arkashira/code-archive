# Business Model Canvas for Code Archive

## Customer Segments
- Individual developers and freelancers seeking reliable backup solutions.
- Small to medium-sized development teams needing collaborative backup and restore functionalities.
- Enterprises with large-scale development operations requiring robust data management.
- Educational institutions teaching software development that need to manage student projects.

## Value Propositions
- Reliable and efficient backup and restore solution tailored for developers.
- Seamless integration with popular development tools (e.g., GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket).
- User-friendly interface that simplifies the backup process.
- Automated backup schedules to minimize data loss risk.
- Competitive pricing model that provides value for both individuals and teams.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the official website.
- Partnerships with development tool platforms for bundled offerings.
- Online marketplaces (e.g., GitHub Marketplace, AWS Marketplace).
- Developer community forums and tech blogs for awareness and promotion.

## Customer Relationships
- Online support through chat and ticketing systems for immediate assistance.
- Community forums for user interaction and feedback.
- Regular updates and newsletters to keep users informed about new features.
- User onboarding sessions and tutorials to enhance user experience.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: Monthly and annual plans starting at $10/month (approx. 350 THB) for individuals and $50/month (approx. 1,750 THB) for teams.
- One-time purchase option for perpetual licenses at $120 (approx. 4,200 THB).
- Affiliate marketing revenue from partnerships with development tool platforms.
- Premium features and add-ons available for an additional fee.

## Key Resources
- Development team skilled in software engineering and DevSecOps practices.
- Cloud infrastructure for hosting backup services and data storage.
- Marketing and sales team to drive customer acquisition.
- Customer support team to assist users and maintain satisfaction.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and improvement of the backup and restore solution.
- Marketing campaigns to raise awareness and attract new customers.
- Customer support and engagement to maintain relationships and gather feedback.
- Regular security audits and updates to ensure data integrity and safety.

## Key Partners
- Cloud service providers (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud) for reliable data storage solutions.
- Development tool platforms for integration and co-marketing opportunities.
- Educational institutions for pilot programs and feedback loops.
- Influencers and tech bloggers for promotional partnerships.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs (salaries, tools, and infrastructure): ~$200,000/year (approx. 7,000,000 THB).
- Marketing and sales expenses: ~$50,000/year (approx. 1,750,000 THB).
- Customer support and operational costs: ~$30,000/year (approx. 1,050,000 THB).
- Cloud storage and hosting fees: ~$20,000/year (approx. 700,000 THB).
