# Customer Journey Map for Code-Archive

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developer experiences data loss or corruption, leading to a realization of the need for a reliable backup solution.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about existing backup solutions; confusion over features and reliability of options available.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, anxiety, fear of losing important work.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content on the importance of backups; testimonials from other developers; free resources or webinars on best practices for data management.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement with educational content.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Developer actively searches for backup solutions after experiencing data loss.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of options; difficulty comparing features and pricing; concerns about integration with existing tools.
- **User Emotions**: Overwhelmed, cautious, skeptical about claims made by different solutions.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear comparison charts; free trials; personalized demos; user-friendly website with detailed FAQs.
- **Metric**: Number of product comparisons made and demo requests.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Developer signs up for a free trial or demo of Code-Archive.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; lack of clear guidance during the trial; potential bugs or performance issues.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful but anxious about the usability and effectiveness of the solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Onboarding support; step-by-step setup guides; responsive customer support during the trial.
- **Metric**: Trial activation rate and user engagement metrics during the trial period.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Developer decides to purchase a subscription after a successful trial.
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about ongoing costs; uncertainty about the long-term value of the product.
- **User Emotions**: Relief from finding a solution, but also apprehension about committing financially.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear communication of value; discounts for early adopters; easy subscription management and cancellation policies.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Developer integrates Code-Archive into their workflow and discovers additional features.
- **Friction Points**: Limited awareness of all features; potential for feature overload; difficulty in scaling the solution for larger teams.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction from effective use, but possible frustration if features are not well communicated.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular feature updates and newsletters; community forums for sharing best practices; referral incentives for team expansions.
- **Metric**: Rate of feature adoption and user retention metrics.
